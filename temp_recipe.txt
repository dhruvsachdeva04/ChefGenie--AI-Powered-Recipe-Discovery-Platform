## Mushroom Biryani

**Ingredients:**

* 1 tbsp oil
* 1 large onion, finely chopped
* 1 inch ginger, finely chopped
* 2 cloves garlic, minced
* 1 tsp green chili paste (adjust to taste)
* 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
* 1 tsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)
* 1 tsp coriander powder
* 1/2 tsp cumin powder
* 1/4 tsp garam masala
* 250g mushrooms, sliced
* 1 cup basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
* 2 cups water or vegetable broth
* 1/2 cup yogurt
* 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
* Salt to taste
* Saffron strands (a pinch, optional)
* Fried onions (optional, for garnish)


**Instructions:**

1. Heat oil in a pan/pot. Sauté onions until golden. Add ginger, garlic, and green chili paste; sauté for a minute.
2. Add turmeric, red chili, coriander, cumin, and garam masala powders; sauté for 30 seconds.
3. Add mushrooms and salt. Cook until mushrooms soften and release water.
4. Add soaked rice, water/broth, and yogurt. Mix well. Bring to a boil.
5. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until rice is cooked.
6. Stir in cilantro and saffron (if using).
7. Garnish with fried onions (if using) and serve hot.
